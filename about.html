Charlyne's Projects Cralyne Njongoro is a skilled cartographer and GIS expert who is passionate about using technology to solve complex spatial problems and help organizations make better decisions. She has a deep understanding of data collection methods, data processing, and data analysis techniques that are used to create maps and other spatial visualizations. She also has a strong foundation in cartography, geography, and computer science.

Interests

Some of my interests include exploring new techniques and tools for data collection and analysis, staying up to date with the latest GIS software and technology, and collaborating with professionals from a variety of fields to solve complex spatial problems. I am also interested in using GIS to improve environmental sustainability, social justice, and public health outcomes.
